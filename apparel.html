Purses, Apparel & Gifts

More than a tack shop. Purses, western apparel and gifts that customers call an underrated find, worth the stop even if you do not own a horse.

Tucked among the tack you will find purses, western apparel and giftable odds and ends that surprise first time visitors. It is the part of the shop that makes R Ranch a destination, not just an errand. Stop in for a gift and you might leave with something for yourself too.

Good for

Gift shoppers, western style lovers, and anyone tagging along who does not ride but still wants to browse.

What to expect

Wander and discover. Ask if you are shopping for a gift and we will help you land on something memorable.

  • Friendly gift suggestions
  • Something for the non riders too
