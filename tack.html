Tack & Riding Gear

The everyday gear that keeps you riding. Wall to wall bridles, bits, reins, halters, lead ropes, cinches, pads and grooming tools, new and gently used.

From a barn full of basics to that one hard to find piece, our tack wall covers the daily needs of real riders. We stock dependable new gear and a rotating selection of quality used tack, so you can outfit a horse without emptying the truck. Not sure what bit or pad you need? Ask. We would rather get it right than just make a sale.

Good for

Boarders and barn owners restocking, new riders building a kit, and anyone hunting a specific bit, halter or pad at a fair price.

What to expect

Come browse the wall. If you are matching a bit or sizing a halter, bring details about your horse and we will help you choose.

What you will find

  • Bridles, headstalls and reins
  • Bits for many disciplines
  • Halters and lead ropes
  • Cinches, pads and blankets
  • Brushes and grooming supplies
  • New and quality used options side by side
