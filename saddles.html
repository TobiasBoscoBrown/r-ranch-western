Saddles, New & Used

The heart of the shop. New and quality used saddles for trail, ranch, barrel, show and that exciting first horse, with honest fitting help and no pressure.

We carry western and all-purpose saddles, new and gently used, across a wide range of prices. Whether you are buying a first saddle for a new horse or stepping up to a show rig, we will look at the horse, the rider and the budget, and point you to saddles that genuinely fit. If we do not have the right one today, we will tell you straight.

Good for

New horse owners, riders upgrading or changing disciplines, parents shopping for a growing kid, and anyone who has been burned by a saddle that did not fit.

What to expect

Plan to spend a little time. We like to talk through your horse and how you ride before pulling saddles. Most saddles bought in store can go home the same day on our try-out so you can check the fit where it matters.

What you will find

  • New and used western and all-purpose saddles
  • Honest fit advice for horse and rider
  • Same day saddle try-out, in store
  • 24 hour, no risk return on in-store purchases if it does not fit
  • Saddle pads, cinches and accessories
  • Help trading or selling your old saddle
